Smart Economics

Nearly half of all fees are systematically destroyed. Burning fees diminishes the coin supply. The logic is straightforward supply and demand: Given steady demand, free markets tend to respond to a dwindling supply with rising asset prices.

VALUE protection

mechanisims

WGR cryptocurrency is designed with a deflationary mechanism that helps the coin retain value under various conditions. During periods of mass adoption, increasing demand would coincide with decreasing supply, creating significant market pressure. As the price increases, per-bet volume is expected to level out, and the rate of deflation should level out, too.

On the other hand, when WGR’s market price drops, the per-bet volume is expected to rise. As net betting volume increases, the burn rate accelerates, increasing the velocity of deflation. In this case, the deflationary mechanism tends to return the WGR price to equilibrium over time.

While WGR’s price is expected to fluctuate, the network’s monetary policy reinforces the cryptocurrency’s price stability.
